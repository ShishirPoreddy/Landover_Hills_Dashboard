How It Works

  1. Ingestion: Budget documents are automatically processed and stored in a structured database
  2. Extraction: AI agents extract fiscal years, departments, line items, and amounts
  3. Validation: Automated checks ensure data accuracy and flag anomalies
  4. Summarization: Natural language summaries are generated for each department/year
  5. Q&A: Residents can ask questions and get direct answers with evidence

Data Sources

The system processes various types of municipal financial documents:

  • Budget ordinances and amendments
  • Annual audit reports
  • Quarterly financial statements
  • Department budget requests
  • Capital project proposals
  • Meeting minutes and resolutions

Privacy & Security

All budget data is public information. The system processes only publicly available municipal documents and does not collect or store personal information. All data processing is performed securely and in compliance with public records laws.
